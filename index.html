HEADING TO CLASS

About the Video

The Video 'Heading To My First Class at The Arts Cente', aims to Showcase the visual Point Of View of a student at NYUAD heading to class. It captures elements of art within the Arts Center that range from the drawing and handicrafts to interior design and architecture.

The Image Gallery

The Image Gallery Showcases other Art Elements and areas in the Arts Center that were not featured in the Film. In other words, 'The Behind The Scenes' of the Film.
